# Elixir Bot - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 11, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Elixir ("the Bot", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot service.

## Information We Collect

### Game Data
We collect and store the following data to provide bot functionality:
- **User ID**: Your Discord user ID to identify your game profile
- **Game Progress**: Fish caught, islands explored, XP, levels, battle pass progress
- **Inventory Data**: Fish inventory, collectibles, equipment, and items
- **Currency**: Seashells and Sun Tokens balances
- **Statistics**: Total fish caught, explorations, challenge completions
- **Timestamps**: When you join, last activity, daily/weekly resets

### Message Content
- **Command Usage**: We read messages that start with our bot prefix (`++` or `!`) to process game commands
- **Command Arguments**: Parameters you provide with commands (e.g., bet amounts, equipment names)
- **We DO NOT** store message content, read messages without our prefix, or monitor general chat conversations

### Server Data
- **Server ID**: To enable server-wide features (boss battles, leaderboards)
- **Server Settings**: Bot configuration and preferences per server

## How We Use Information

We use collected data solely to:
- Provide game functionality and features
- Save and track your game progress
- Enable multiplayer features (trading, boss battles, leaderboards)
- Improve bot performance and user experience
- Prevent abuse and maintain fair gameplay

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely in encrypted databases
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
- Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only
- We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties

## Data Retention

- **Active Users**: Data is retained as long as you actively use the bot
- **Inactive Users**: Data may be deleted after extended periods of inactivity (6+ months)
- **Deletion Requests**: You can request data deletion at any time (see Your Rights below)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your stored data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using the bot to discontinue data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us through our support server or via the contact information below.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Discord API**: To operate as a Discord bot
- **MongoDB/Database Service**: To store game data
- These services have their own privacy policies and terms of service

## Children's Privacy

Our bot is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the data immediately.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be announced in our support server. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Message Content Intent Usage

We use Discord's Message Content Intent solely for:
- Reading messages that begin with our bot prefixes (`++` or `!`)
- Parsing command names and arguments
- Providing game functionality through prefix commands

We do not:
- Store or log message content
- Read messages without bot prefixes
- Monitor or analyze general conversations
- Use message data for advertising or training AI/ML models
